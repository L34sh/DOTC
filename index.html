ABOUT CORDOVA

A Municipality Rich in History and Natural Beauty!

Cordova, officially the Municipality of Cordova, is a municipality in the province of Cebu, Philippines with a population of 72,915 people (2024 census). Established as a municipality in 1864, Cordova consists of a main island and several islets including Gilutongan, Nalusuan, Shell, Tongo and Lava. Located 18 kilometers from Lapu-Lapu City and 28 kilometers from Cebu City, Cordova is connected to mainland Cebu via the newly opened Cebu-Cordova Link Expressway (completed in 2022), making it easily accessible for visitors. The municipality is known for its flat limestone terrain with the highest point being only 10 meters above sea level, pristine beaches, mangrove forests, and vibrant local culture that offers visitors an authentic Filipino island experience.
